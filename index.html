About Me

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented data enthusiast with a solid foundation in mathematics and hands-on experience in data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. I hold an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Banaras Hindu University and have strengthened my skill set through advanced certifications from IBM, Google, and IIT Roorkee, along with practical experience gained through internships and job simulations with leading organizations.

My technical skills span across Python, R, SQL, and key data science libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn, complemented by proficiency in deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, Keras, and PyTorch. I am also well-versed in big data technologies including Hadoop and Spark, enabling me to tackle complex datasets and derive actionable insights.

Throughout my projects and professional training, I have successfully developed end-to-end machine learning models, performed sentiment analysis, built AI-powered chatbots, and executed data visualization tasks that inform strategic decision-making.

With a passion for transforming data into meaningful stories and driving business impact, I am eager to contribute my analytical mindset and technical expertise to challenging data-driven roles.
